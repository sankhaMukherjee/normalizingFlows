Important Theorems and Lemmas
==============================


1. Matrix Determinant Lemma
-----------------------------

.. math::
    
    \det (\mathbf I + \mathbf u \mathbf v^T ) = 
    (1 + \mathbf v^T \mathbf u)

2. Mean Field Approximation
-----------------------------

    p(A,B) = p(A)p(B)
